# Contributing Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the ICES Transparent Assessment Framework (TAF) Governance group repository. This repository is used to support discussion, coordination, and documentation related to the governance of TAF.

Contributions, comments, and questions are welcome and encouraged, provided they follow the guidance below.

---

## Code of Conduct

All participants are expected to engage respectfully and constructively. Please be considerate of differing perspectives and remember that this repository serves an international and interdisciplinary community.

---

## Creating Issues

Issues are the primary mechanism for:
- Raising questions related to TAF governance
- Proposing changes or improvements
- Reporting problems with documentation or processes
- Requesting clarification or discussion

When creating an issue:
- Use a clear and descriptive title
- Provide enough context for others to understand the topic
- Be concise and focused on a single topic where possible
- Indicate whether the issue is a question, proposal, or discussion item

---

## Commenting on Issues

Comments on issues are welcome and encouraged.

When commenting:
- Stay on topic and keep discussions constructive
- Provide rationale or references where appropriate
- Avoid duplicating points already made unless adding new context
- Be respectful of differing views and experiences

Governance discussions may involve iteration over time; revisiting and refining ideas is expected.

---

## Sensitive and Personal Information

This is a **public** GitHub repository.

Please **do not**:
- Share personal data about yourself or others (e.g. email addresses, phone numbers, private affiliations)
- Share confidential, sensitive, or non-public information
- Upload assessment data, working documents, or materials that are not explicitly approved for public release
- Reference internal discussions or decisions that are not already public

If you are unsure whether something is appropriate to share publicly, err on the side of caution.

---

## Scope of Contributions

This repository focuses on **governance-related topics** for the ICES Transparent Assessment Framework. Technical support questions, bug reports, or code issues related to specific TAF repositories should be raised in the relevant project repository instead.

---

## Questions

If you are unsure where or how to raise a topic, feel free to open an issue asking for guidance.

Thank you for helping to keep TAF governance transparent, constructive, and collaborative.
